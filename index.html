01 · About the Client

Who is 420.com?

420.com is a global lifestyle and content platform in the cannabis space, serving a wide audience interested in cannabis culture, education, and related products. Operating in a highly competitive and restrictive niche, the brand relies heavily on organic search as its primary growth engine — making SEO a critical priority.

Despite having an established domain and recognizable brand name, the website was significantly underperforming in organic search when the engagement began.

02 · The Challenge

Key problems to solve

The site had clear potential — an established domain, a recognizable name — but no coherent SEO foundation to capitalize on it. Six core issues needed to be addressed simultaneously.

Zero traffic visibility

Low organic traffic despite an established domain. The site wasn't ranking for any meaningful commercial or informational terms.

Weak keyword strategy

No structured approach to capturing search demand. Missing keyword targeting and content alignment across the funnel.

Poor internal linking

Inadequate internal structure made it difficult for search engines to discover and index pages efficiently.

No AI search presence

Complete absence in AI-driven tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity — a growing and critical discovery channel.

Technical debt

Crawl errors, redirect chains, and indexing issues slowing performance and limiting search engine trust signals.

Authority gap

A weak backlink profile relative to competitors, limiting the ability to rank for competitive cannabis terms.

03 · My Approach

Building a connected ecosystem

The strategy focused on compounding actions — not isolated fixes. Every layer of work was designed to reinforce the next, creating sustainable momentum across all organic channels.

01
On-Page SEO Optimization
  • Optimized meta titles, descriptions, and headings for target keywords and click-through rate
  • Improved keyword placement and readability across all key pages
  • Enhanced internal linking between pages and search engines
02
Keyword Strategy & Expansion
  • Targeted long-tail, intent-driven keywords to capture qualified traffic
  • Expanded keyword coverage across key pages and content clusters
  • Built topical authority silos around core cannabis themes
03
Internal Linking & Structure
  • Strengthened internal linking framework for better crawl efficiency
  • Ensured topical authority signals across all key pages
  • Enhanced crawlability and navigation for both users and bots
04
Technical SEO Foundations
  • Fixed indexing and crawlability issues causing visibility loss
  • Resolved redirect chains and ensured key pages were properly discoverable
  • Improved overall site architecture and URL structure
05
AI Search Optimization (AEO)
  • Structured content for AI-driven search visibility across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Mode
  • Included topical depth and authority signals across all content
  • Increased probability of being cited in AI responses and answer engines

  • 31-minute average engagement time indicating high content relevance
  • High traffic quality, not just volume

  • Organic Search: 4,600+ users with 84.8% engagement rate
  • Referral: 3,900+ users (27× site visits) supporting authority signals
  • Direct: 400+ visitors reflecting growing brand recognition
  • Email & Organic Shopping: Exceptionally high engagement time
Search & Authority Metrics
  • 221+ ranking keywords across competitive clusters
  • 54% U.S. traffic share establishing strong local authority

Compounding Growth Pattern
  • Each improvement amplified the results of previous work

The biggest impact came from aligning search intent, content structure, and site architecture simultaneously — creating a compounding system where improvements in one area amplified all others.
